<h1>Finance Analytics Dashboard for Aviation</h1>


<h2>Description</h2>
This project delivers an interactive finance and revenue analytics dashboard designed to track and analyze invoiced sales revenue within an aviation business environment. The solution consolidates invoicing and sales data to provide clear visibility into billed revenue, payment status, revenue trends, and customer performance across reporting periods. The dashboard enables finance and business stakeholders to monitor revenue recognized through invoiced sales, analyze variances over time, and identify key drivers impacting cash flow and financial performance. Users can drill down by customer, aircraft program, route, service type, invoice status, and time period, supporting accurate financial reporting, revenue forecasting, and data-driven decision-making.
